03 / Principles

Power, with
a way back.

System utilities should earn trust through behavior. MacCleaner keeps destructive choices visible, bounded, and reversible wherever macOS allows.

01

Trash means Trash.

User-selected files move through macOS Trash. If that fails, MacCleaner stops.

Reversible by design
02

Private means local.

Photo similarity and system analysis happen on the Mac, without remote classification.

On-device analysis
03

Deep does not mean endless.

Heavy scans have explicit budgets, cancellation, and honest inspection limits.

Bounded workloads
04

Advice is not control.

Memory recommendations never silently kill apps or force privileged purge operations.
